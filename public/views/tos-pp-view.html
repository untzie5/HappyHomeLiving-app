The app
Happy Home Living is an app for people who live together. It does not matter if it is only 2 people living together or more. This app is suitable for families, friends, roommates, spouses or couples. The purpose of this app is to make your home organized by being able to have a shared to do list, calendar/schedule, shopping list and being able to communicate without sending text messages about small matters. You can write small comments on a task with a small explanation or giving your roommate a heads up about being late from work. This way, it is easier to organize a living situation and help each other out and always stay up to date.
